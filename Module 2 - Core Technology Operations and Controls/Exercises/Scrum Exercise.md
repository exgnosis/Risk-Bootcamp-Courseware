# Scrum Exercise

The purpose of this exercise is to demonstrate the basics of a Scrum process.

The Project will be our previous Thanksgiving dinner project

## Roles

### The Product Owner

- This role represents the interests of the stakeholders
- Task: Identify who the stakeholders are

### The Scrum Master

- The person making sure that everything is being done correctly
- Not a manager, but rather an advisor and arbitrator

### Team Members

- The individuals actually doing the work.

## Artifacts

### The Product Backlog

- The list of features requested
- Sort of a "to do" list
- Has to be groomed, which means prioritized and evaluated

### The Sprint Backlog

- Items to be done during a Sprint

## Ceremonies 

### Project Planning

- Create a sprint schedule with deliverables
- Each sprint moves us one step closer to the final goal

### Sprint Planning

- What we are going to accomplish in the current sprint
- Risks and Quality goals

### Daily Standup

- Daily checkin for status updates

### Sprint Review

- Evaluate the results of the Sprint


### Spring Retrospective

- Review of how well we did, improvements that can be made in the process
